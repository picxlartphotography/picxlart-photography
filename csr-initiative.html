CSR Initiative – Dream Wedding Support Program

At Picxlart Photography & Events Co., we believe that every bride deserves the opportunity to preserve the memories of her special day, regardless of financial circumstances.

As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Initiative, we select up to three deserving brides every year for wedding photography support. This initiative is designed for families who genuinely wish to preserve their wedding memories but are unable to afford professional photography services due to financial limitations.

Who Can Apply?

  • Families with limited financial resources.
  • Brides whose families are unable to afford professional wedding photography despite having a genuine desire to preserve their wedding memories.
  • Families planning a wedding with a modest budget.
  • Applicants who value and respect the importance of photography as a lifelong memory.

Selection Process

Every application is carefully reviewed by our team. Selection may be based on:


  • Family income and financial condition.
  • Overall family background.
  • Wedding budget and circumstances.
  • Supporting information provided by the applicant.
  • Internal verification conducted by Picxlart Photography & Events Co.

Important Note

Submission of an application does not guarantee selection. Only up to three (3) applicants may be selected in a calendar year. Picxlart Photography & Events Co. reserves the sole right to approve, reject, shortlist, or discontinue any application based on internal review and eligibility criteria.
Our objective is simple — to help deserving families preserve one of the most important days of their lives through meaningful and professional wedding photography.
