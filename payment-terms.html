Payment Terms

Last Updated: June 2026

At Picxlart Photography & Events Co., we strive to maintain a transparent, professional, and secure payment process for all our clients. By making any payment, confirming a booking, or using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the following Payment Terms.

1. Booking Confirmation

  • A booking is considered confirmed only after receiving the required advance payment.
  • Dates will not be reserved without advance payment confirmation.

2. Advance Payment

  • A minimum of 50% advance payment is required to confirm a booking.
  • Advance requirements may vary for destination weddings, customized packages, or special assignments.

3. Balance Payment

  • The remaining balance amount must be paid on or before the event day.
  • No final deliverables will be released until full payment is received.

4. Accepted Payment Methods

  • UPI Payments
  • Google Pay
  • PhonePe
  • Paytm
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash (where applicable)

5. Non-Refundable Advance

  • Advance payments are generally non-refundable once a booking has been confirmed.
  • Any exception remains solely at the discretion of Picxlart Photography & Events Co.

6. Date Change / Rescheduling

  • Clients must notify us as early as possible regarding any change of date.
  • Rescheduling requests are subject to availability.
  • Additional charges may apply depending on circumstances.

7. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation after booking confirmation does not guarantee any refund.
  • Any approved refund shall remain solely at the company's discretion.

8. Additional Charges

  • Extra coverage hours
  • Additional photographers or videographers
  • Destination or outstation assignments
  • Album upgrades
  • Special editing requests
  • Additional deliverables

9. Late Payment

  • Late payments may result in delays in delivery.
  • The company reserves the right to withhold deliverables until payment obligations are fulfilled.

10. Payment Disputes

  • Any payment-related concerns should be communicated directly to our management team.
  • Both parties agree to attempt resolution professionally and in good faith.

11. Security of Online Payments

  • Online payments are processed through third-party providers.
  • We do not store debit card details, credit card details, banking passwords, or UPI PINs.

12. Agreement

  • By making any payment, the client confirms that they have read, understood, and agreed to these Payment Terms.
