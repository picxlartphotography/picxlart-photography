Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Picxlart Photography & Events Co., your privacy is important to us.

We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us while using our website, communicating through WhatsApp, social media platforms, email, phone calls, or while booking any of our photography and videography services.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have regarding your personal information.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Event Details
  • Wedding / Event Date
  • Location Information
  • Photographs and Videos captured during assignments
  • Information submitted through enquiry or contact forms

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Responding to enquiries and booking requests
  • Managing photography and videography assignments
  • Client communication and support
  • Delivering photographs, videos, albums, and related services
  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Internal business and record-keeping purposes

3. Photographs & Portfolio Usage

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Picxlart Photography & Events Co. may use selected photographs and videos from assignments for portfolio display, website galleries, social media promotion, advertising, and marketing purposes.

If a client wishes to keep their photographs completely private, a written request must be made before the assignment.

4. Payment Information

We do not store debit card, credit card, banking passwords, UPI PINs, or any sensitive payment credentials.

Payments made through UPI, bank transfer, or third-party payment platforms are processed by their respective providers.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared when required to deliver contracted services, comply with legal obligations, or protect our legal rights and business interests.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect client information, photographs, videos, and project-related files from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no method of online transmission or digital storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites, social media platforms, Google Maps, WhatsApp, or other third-party services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external platforms.

8. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to improve website performance and understand visitor activity.

These tools do not provide us with sensitive personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended to collect personal information directly from children without parental or guardian involvement.
